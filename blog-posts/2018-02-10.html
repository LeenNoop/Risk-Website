How To Turn a Group Into a Team: Reflection

  • Date
    February 10, 2018
  • Posted by
    Daniel Sutton

After our groups embarrassment at the MVP presentation we paused for the holidays and when we returned for teaching period 2 things only got worse as more deadlines approached.

As part of this project we each had to write an individual reflection based on an experience we had as part of our group during our first term. We could all agreed there had been no real progress made and therefore the only concrete experience is that as a group of individuals we have struggled to work as a team.

This should be about reflecting on ourselves as individuals to think about why this situation has occurred and what could each of us could do to improve the group dynamic.

My personal reflection has given me a goal to turn this group into a team and with the help of all our reflections it be achieved. Hopefully our reflections affect our group members so we can begin to work as a team and perhaps our next post will be about some progress on the game.
